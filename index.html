About

Welcome to Océane Hair Studio, Océane has over 14 experience in hair industry and was trained in France before coming to Australia in 2017.

Founded with a passion for health, sustainability and a love for the environment, our salon offers a unique and intimate experience like no other.

This is not just a place to get your hair done, but a sanctuary where you can escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and reconnect with the tranquillity of the forest.

As a one-on-one salon, we pride ourselves on providing personalized attention and care to each client. You'll never feel rushed or overlooked here—instead, you'll receive our undivided attention as we work together to create a look that enhances your natural beauty and reflects your individual style.

We exclusively use organic, eco-friendly hair care products that are gentle on both your hair and the planet. Step into our salon and experience the serenity of the forest, the warmth of personalized service, and the rejuvenating power of organic beauty.
